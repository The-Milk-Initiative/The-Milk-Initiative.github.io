Bantuan Susu Formula Bayi.

The Milk Initiative (Inisiatif Susu) menawarkan susu formula bayi percuma kepada keluarga berkeperluan di Kuala Lumpur dan Petaling Jaya.

Proses Permohonan Susu

Ambil perhatian
Oleh kerana inisiatif ini masih disempurnakan dan mempunyai dana yang terhad, The Milk Initiative hanya menawarkan susu formula percuma untuk:
  • Anak kecil berumur 12 bulan dan ke bawah;
  • Maksimum 2 anak kecil bagi setiap isi rumah;
  • Anak kecil yang menetap di Kuala Lumpur dan Petaling Jaya.

Borang Permohonan Susu 

The Milk Initiative’s heart is to feed babies whose families genuinely cannot afford to, while looking to ease their financial burden at the same time.

The Milk Initiative believes that no baby should ever go hungry, regardless of race, faith and background.

Who runs The Milk Initiative?

Hi, I’m Joyce, a designer and a fellow Malaysian. Currently it is just me!

What’s the story behind The Milk Initiative?

With a little child at home, I can relate to how stressful it can be when your baby is hungry and there’s nothing to eat.

The idea for this project started years ago, after reading in the local paper about a father who was jailed for stealing baby formula to feed his child. That stuck with me for a very long time, and compelled me to do something to help.

How is The Milk Initiative funded?

With my personal savings! The Milk Initiative does not run on donations from others.
